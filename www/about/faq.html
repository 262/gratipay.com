FAQ

What is Gittip?
Gittip is a platform for sustainable crowd-funding. Use it to set up a $1 per week (or more) anonymous gift to someone you think does great work.
Who is Gittip?
Gittip is an open company, founded by Chad Whitacre. Over a dozen people are contributing code, and more than 40 participate in discussions on GitHub, not to mention Hacker News and other forums.
How is Gittip funded?
Gittip is funded on Gittip.
Can I make a one-time tip?
No. Gittip is designed for long-term, sustainable funding, and only supports recurring gifts at this time.
I’m not in the U.S., can I still tip using Gittip?
Absolutely! We’re able to accept international credit card payments. If you encounter any issues tipping and you are outside the US, please let us know.
I’m not in the U.S., can I receive tips using Gittip?
Yes. But, unfortunately, you can’t automatically withdraw your gifts from Gittip unless you have a U.S. bank account. We’re looking for a solution for non-US withdrawals.
How do I withdraw money from Gittip?
Participants in the U.S. can connect a bank account to have funds deposited weekly (less a $0.30 fee). It takes one business day for deposits to clear, so generally they will clear on Friday. Outside the U.S., payouts are a manual process (with postage and/or fees charged at cost).
Can I pay with bitcoin?
No. The challenges with bitcoin are handling recurring payments and transparently converting to USD. We are currently seeking help to implement bitcoin tips.
Can I use Gittip to support organizations?
Yes. Some accounts on Gittip represent organizations.
How do I know my tips are being received by the right person?
You can follow links to Gittip from a trusted source like a person or organization’s website. Also, Twitter verifies accounts in instances where this is particularly relevant, so check their Twitter account carefully if it’s linked.
Do I have to pay taxes on the income I receive from Gittip?
Gittip is operating on the principle that money received through the site is a gift for tax purposes, in jurisdictions where that is relevant.
Are gifts I give through Gittip tax-deductible?
No.
I don’t want to receive tips, I just want to give them. What should I do?
Please select “I’m not here to receive tips. I’ll generally regift them” as your personal funding goal.
